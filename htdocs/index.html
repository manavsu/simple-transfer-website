How to download

Method A (on a computer):

  1. Click on the link below.
  2. Right click on the file you want to download (or left click, but your own from there).
  3. Left click on the image/video and save.

Method B (also on a computer):

  1. Open the terminal/powershell.
  2. Run wget --http-user={USR} --http-password={PWD} -r http://www.mammothisfun.duckdns.org/videos/ to download everything.
  3. Run wget --http-user={USR} --http-password={PWD} http://www.mammothisfun.duckdns.org/videos/{FILENAME} to download a specific file.
